GALLERIA AT ROSEVILLE TO HOST ROSEVILLE 2000
Kim Delgado and Naaz Alikhan named co-chairs of annual business exposition


ROSEVILLE - The Roseville Chamber of Commerce and Urban Retail Properties Co. today announced that Galleria at Roseville would host Roseville 2000 next July. The daylong area economic development showcase dates back to 1986 and is one of the Chamber's biggest fundraisers. Galleria at Roseville, a state-of-the-art 1.1 million square foot regional shopping center anchored by Nordstrom, Macy's, JCPenney and Sears, is currently under construction and will open August 25, 2000. Roseville 2000 will serve as a community "sneak preview" of the Galleria, which is sure to become one of the pillars of the area's already-powerful economy.

"We are thrilled to host Roseville 2000 at Galleria at Roseville," said Wendy Gerig, Roseville Chamber of Commerce CEO. "The Galleria is one of Roseville's most exciting economic development success stories, and a fitting backdrop for this event, which is dedicated to highlighting our community's vibrant entrepreneurial spirit."

Urban Retail Properties Co., the management company for Galleria at Roseville, noted that although they do not normally host community functions at their facilities during the construction phase, they would make an exception in this instance because Roseville 2000 is such an important event representing what is a rapidly-growing, dynamic business community. As a result, both sides are working together to make this event possible next July.

"We appreciate the Roseville Chamber of Commerce's willingness to work with us so that we could make the necessary arrangements to host Roseville 2000 at the Galleria," said Jan Porter, Urban Retail Properties Co. Senior Vice President. "We are committed to becoming part of the fabric that is the Roseville business community and excited to be able to contribute to this event."

Tasked with organizing and managing Roseville 2000, Kim Delgado and Naaz Alikhan were named co-chairs of the event. Ms. Delgado is the owner of Blossoms and Balloons and Ms. Alikhan is a space planner with the architectural firm Williams and Paddon.

"Our goal has always been to put on an event that builds upon the previous year, and with Galleria at Roseville and our dynamic co-chairs, Roseville 2000 will be an event to remember. You can bet on that," concluded Gerig.
