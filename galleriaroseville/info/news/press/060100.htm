GALLERIA AT ROSEVILLE ANNOUNCES
APPAREL AND SPECIALTY STORE LINEUP
J. Crew, Abercrombie & Fitch, J. Jill, American Eagle Outfitters, Guess?, Ann Taylor and Borders Books Music & Cafe to open on August 25th


ROSEVILLE - Urban Shopping Centers, Inc. (NYSE: URB) announced today 57 of the 120 apparel and specialty merchants that will open at Galleria at Roseville, the 1.1 million square foot regional shopping center scheduled to open August 25, 2000. Anchored by Nordstrom, Macy's, JCPenney and Sears, the Galleria will offer shoppers a wide selection of men's and women's apparel; footwear; home furnishings; sporting goods and other specialty items. Galleria at Roseville will be the first Sacramento area regional shopping center to include an adjacent outdoor cityscape, called the Promenade, which will feature several upscale restaurants and distinctive two-level retail stores.

"Galleria at Roseville will have an exceptional lineup of apparel and specialty stores, including several that are new to the Sacramento area," said Ross B. Glickman, president-leasing for Urban Retail Properties Co. "J. Crew, Abercrombie & Fitch, J. Jill and Ann Taylor are just a few of the places shoppers can find the newest and finest merchandise."

NEW RETAILERS
J. Jill, renowned for its catalog sales, will open its ninth store nationwide and first in California at the Galleria. The store will feature high-quality women's apparel, accessories, shoes and gifts.

Footwear retailers Steve Madden and Stone & Co. will open their first Sacramento area stores at the Galleria. Steve Madden will feature a contemporary line of women's casual, athletic and evening shoes and accessories, while Stone & Co. will offer distinctive men's and women's shoes and accessories.

Galleria at Roseville will also unveil a variety of unique gift and specialty stores. Brighton Collectibles, opening their first Sacramento area store, will offer a wide selection of upscale jewelry, belts and accessories. Swatch will offer their collection of distinctive Swiss watches and accessories. Mayor's Jewelers and Helzberg Diamonds will feature fine jewelry and watches for men and women.

Several health and beauty stores also make their area debut at Galleria at Roseville. Aveda will offer all natural skincare, haircare and body treatment products. Origins will feature aromatic oils and botanical extracts in their collection of skin care, bath, body, lifestyle and sensory therapy products for men, women and even pets. Bare Escentuals will bring a unique concept of beauty to Galleria at Roseville through hundreds of innovative and exclusive formulas for body, hair and face using pure and natural ingredients.

OTHER DISTINCTIVE RETAILERS
Galleria at Roseville will include several prominent apparel retailers reflecting today's "casual outdoor" fashions including American Eagle Outfitters and Abercrombie & Fitch, whose store will reflect their contemporary "canoe" design. Eddie Bauer will also offer distinctive clothing and accessories for today's active and casual lifestyle. The Finish Line will feature outdoor sporting goods, apparel, eyewear and accessories for the sporting enthusiasts.

Other upscale Galleria clothing stores include Guess? which will feature its trend-setting collection of denim clothing and accessories for adults, children and the home. J. Crew will offer casual clothing and accessories and contemporary fashions for the young professional's workweek wardrobe.

Women are the focus of several Galleria at Roseville apparel merchants. Ann Taylor will feature the store's signature classic fashions for today's professional woman, while bebe will present its distinctive line of apparel and accessories appealing to the hip, adventurous woman.

As one of the leading specialty retailers, Brookstone - featuring functional and unique gifts and gadgets - will open one of their "next generation prototype" stores at the Galleria. One of only five in the world, it will feature their new design, unique fixtures and innovative customer displays.

THE PROMENADE
Galleria at Roseville's signature Promenade - an outdoor pedestrian streetscape featuring upscale restaurants and distinctive retailers - will include Borders Books Music & Cafe with a 25,120 square foot two-level store offering a wide variety of books and music, as well as a full-service espresso bar, salads, sandwiches and pastries. Copeland's Sports will also open a 31,164 square foot two-level Promenade store where athletes will find a broad spectrum of sporting equipment and apparel. The Galleria location will reflect Copeland's "superstore" design, offering six distinct merchandising concepts: Golf, fitness, outdoors, athletics, apparel and footwear.

Galleria at Roseville is owned and developed by Chicago-based Urban Shopping Centers, Inc. (NYSE: URB), a self-administered real estate investment trust (REIT), which is in the business of owning, acquiring, managing, leasing, developing and redeveloping super-regional and regional malls. The company opened Brandon TownCenter in Tampa, Florida in February 1995 and Wolfchase Galleria in Memphis, Tennessee in February 1997, a second Tampa-area mall, Citrus Park Town Center, in March 1999, and expects to open Galleria at Roseville on August 25, 2000 and The Streets at Southpoint in Durham, North Carolina in spring 2002. Urban Shopping Centers, Inc. owns interests in several of the premier shopping centers in the United States including Oakbrook Center (Oak Brook, Illinois), Century City Shopping Center & Marketplace (Los Angeles, California), Water Tower Place (Chicago, Illinois), Old Orchard Center (Skokie, Illinois), Copley Place (Boston, Massachusetts),San Francisco Shopping Centre (San Francisco, California), and The Houston Galleria (Houston, Texas), as well as in Urban Retail Properties Co., its property management, leasing and development affiliate. Urban Retail Properties Co. is one of the nation's largest retail property managers, managing more than 59 million square feet of space in 26 states and the District of Columbia.
