From its very inception, Galleria at Roseville was designed to a higher standard. From the Italian tile floors and spectacular Promenade, with its outdoor fireplace, bronze sculptures, and outdoor cafe-style seating... to the children's play park and gallery displaying the ceramic art of local kids... from the more than 120 stores and restaurants... to the thoughtful amenities like baby-changing rooms and strollers... this is a different kind of shopping venue. Galleria at Roseville is a unique selection of the finest and most stylish.
It's a place where you'll find both fine dining and quick cuisine. A place that doesn't merely accommodate children, but celebrates them. A place where the whole family can enjoy a shopping experience unparalleled in Sacramento - or most other American cities, for that matter. A place that is literally redefining style. So browse, explore, enjoy. Between stores and restaurants that are familiar favorites and the wealth of trendsetting newcomers, we've no doubt you'll find many destinations that are just your style.
